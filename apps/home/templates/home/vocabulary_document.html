Vocabulary Game

Vocabulary is the foundation of English. Without a good vocabulary, if you have enough knowledge of grammer, you cannot read and listen to anything. Besides you cannot write or speak your ideas. To increase your vocabulary, your English skills improve in every way.

In this game, we use the brain process of memorization.

Your brain lost memory about you remember over time. Things you just remember at first are forgotten rapidly. But if you remember again before you completely forget it, the speed of forgetting is reduced. By repeating this process, your memory become strong and finally you will never forget it.

The system manages all learning words. In the first period of a learning word, the word appears frequently to make strong memorization. So you can easily remember the word. As time proceeds, the frequency reduces. Because you remember the word in this phase, it's no problem. Finally, you remember the word.

In this game, all words are separated into 'known' or 'unknown' state. And all 'unknown' words are separated into 5 state. First, the unknown word is classified as 'state 1', which means it's the first period of memorizing process. If you answer this word correctly, then the word state becomes 2 and it will appears after 1 day. If you also answer the word, the state increases to 3 and it will appears after 2 day. As the state increases, the duration between appearing gets longer. Finally, you remember the word.

How to Play

1. Select a package
Packages are organized in 5 levels. You can choose one suite for you.
2. Select unknowing words in the package
To remember only unknowning words, you judge which words you have to learn. In this page, you can use arrow keys: left arrow key to mark the word as known, right arrow key to mark the word as unknown and down arrow key to undo operation.
3. Review state 1 words
Before starting game, you can review state 1 words to make sure you know all words appearing at first.
4. Start game
In game page, you answer the correct word from the meaning written in your selected language.
