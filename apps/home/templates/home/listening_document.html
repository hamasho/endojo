Listening Game

Can you understand all words in English converstation? Or you miss some words and guess the missed words from other words you could have heard, don't you?

In such situation, it depends on lack whether you can understand the meaning of the spoken sentences. If there are many missed words, you cannot understand what he/she wants to say. You can talk only when most of spoken words are simple and easy to understant. For example, if you cannot distinguish 'r' pronunciation, you are confused with any words including 'r'.

In this game, we reduce such unclear words by hearing sentences repeatedly until you understand every word completely. All unclear words become clear and your listening skill will be improved. Your speaking skill will be improved also, by knowing the right pronunciation.
