Listening Game

Can you understand all words in English converstation? You miss some words and imagine the missed words from other words you have heard, don't you?

In such situation, it depends on lack whether you can understand the meaning of the spoken sentences. If you cannot distinguish 'r' pronunciation, you feel confusion with all words including 'r'.

In this game, we reduce such unclear words by hearing sentences repeatedly until you understand every word clearly. All unclear words become clear and your listening skill will be improved. Your speaking skill will be improved also, by knowing the right pronunciation.
